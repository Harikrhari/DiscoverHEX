## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

---

# The Market Is Broken. Here's How.

---

### Problem 1: The Market Is Fragmented

Consumers who care about fitness, wellness, adventure, and high-performance living shop across 10–15 disconnected platforms. There is **no single premium destination** for the human excellence lifestyle.

> A person training for their first marathon shops Amazon for shoes, a random Shopify store for supplements, another site for recovery tools, and YouTube for advice — with zero cohesion, zero brand loyalty, and zero impact.

### Problem 2: The Dropship Stigma Is Killing a Viable Model

Direct-to-consumer sourced product businesses generate billions annually. But the sector is plagued by **cheap branding, no curation standards, and zero accountability.** The result: consumers distrust sourced goods even when quality is high.

> **The problem isn't the supply chain. It's the absence of a brand.**

### Problem 3: Charity Is Opaque and Performative

In 2025, US consumers donated $557B to charity. Yet 73% of millennials report **distrust in how donated money is actually used.** Major brands slap "We Give Back" on their checkout — and provide zero proof.

> Consumers want to know: which cause, how much, and what happened.

### Problem 4: Creators Are Undervalued and Fragmented

The global influencer marketing market hit **$24B in 2025**. Yet most creators earn 1–5% commissions through generic affiliate programs, with no community identity, no guaranteed brand alignment, and no upside beyond per-click pennies.

### Problem 5: Consumers Have No Stake in the Brands They Love

Every platform the modern consumer loves — Amazon, Instagram, YouTube — extracts value from their attention and loyalty. **None of them offer consumers equity.**

---

**Speaker Notes:**
These five problems are not hypothetical — they are documented consumer behavior patterns. We're not solving one problem. We're solving the cluster of problems that defines the modern lifestyle commerce experience. Each problem is also a revenue opportunity for DiscoverHEX.
